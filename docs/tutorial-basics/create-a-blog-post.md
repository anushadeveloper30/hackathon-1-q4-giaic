# Create a Physical AI Post

In this book framework, you can create a **post for each topic** in Physical AI. Each post can have its own **index**, **tags**, and even serve as reference for other chapters.

## Create your first Post

Create a file at `blog/2025-12-10-introduction.md`:

```md title="blog/2025-12-10-introduction.md"
---
slug: introduction
title: Introduction to Physical AI
authors:
  - name: Anusha Akhter
    title: Author of Physical AI
    url: https://github.com/yourusername
    image_url: https://github.com/yourusername.png
tags: [introduction, basics]
---

Welcome to the world of Physical AI! This post introduces the key concepts of **embodied intelligence**, highlighting the difference between software-based AI and AI that interacts with the physical world.

You will learn why **a body is essential for true intelligence**, how sensors and movement shape learning, and how this field is opening new frontiers in AI research.

Explore, experiment, and expand your understanding of how AI moves from digital minds to physical beings.
